Who am I?

My name is Peyman Mahmoudi, I am an experienced front-end developer & user experience designer. Mainly focusing on responsive layouts, HTML5, JavaScript, CSS3, UX, Prototyping.
I like the diversity of web technologies and I am excited about the continuous evolvement of front-end languages and programming styles.

Next to my technical skills, I am an enthusiastic, loyal and proactive person and eager to learn.
I think it is important to work in an inspiring team, in which good communication and sharing knowledge is common.
